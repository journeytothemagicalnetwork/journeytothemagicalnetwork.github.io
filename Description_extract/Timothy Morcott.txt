"Timothy Morcott was a wizard and Slytherin student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s.During the 1996-1997 school year, he stood in front of a door at one end of the Stone Bridge, forcing Harry Potter to beat him in a duel in order to get past. He might be the same student that stood in front of Harry in the Training Grounds to get past.The name \"Timothy\" comes from the Greek name  (Timotheos) meaning \"honouring God\", derived from  (timao) \"to honour\" and  (theos) \"god\".Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (First appearance)Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game) (NDS version only)"
