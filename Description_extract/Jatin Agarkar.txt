"Jatin Agarkar was a Ravenclaw student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He was seen walking around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.\"Jatin\" is a name for Shiva, and means \"He who has matted locks\". Agarkar is an Indian surname most commonly found in the Maharashtra state.This student was only featured in the PlayStation 2 version of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban.Based on his name, it can be presumed that he is of Indian descent.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (PS2 version only)"
