"Craig Dunn was a Hufflepuff student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He competed in the school Gobstones tournament during the 19961997 school year.The name \"Craig\" is from a Scottish surname which was derived from Gaelic creag meaning \"crag\" or \"rocks\".
