"Andrew Snowy Owl was a wizard and Slytherin student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1940s and 1950s. He was Captain of the Slytherin Quidditch team from 1948 to 1952.The name \"Andrew\" comes from the Greek name  (Andreas), which was derived from  (aner) \"man\" (genitive  (andros) \"of a man\"). \"Snowy Owl\" refers to the species of owl.This character is a presumably a tribute to Andrew Ackland-Snow, who worked as an art director on all eight Harry Potter films.Harry Potter films (Name appears on plaque)"
