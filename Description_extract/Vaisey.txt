"Vaisey was a wizard and student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and who sorted into the Slytherin house.He played Chaser for the Slytherin Quidditch team during the 19961997 school year. The day before Slytherin's match against Gryffindor, Vaisey was hit in the head by a bludger and was unable to play.In the film adaption, Vaisey is only seen during the Quidditch match (the first Slytherin who tries to score a goal by hitting the Quaffle into the hoop).Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (First appearance)Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game)"
