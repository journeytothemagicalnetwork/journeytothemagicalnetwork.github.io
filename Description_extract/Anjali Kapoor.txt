"Anjali Kapoor was a Gryffindor student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. She was seen walking around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.Anjali is an Indian name of Sanskrit origin, it means \"homage\" or \"tribute\". Kapoor is a North Indian surname, widely found in the state of Punjab.Based on her name, she is most likely of Indian descent.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (PS2 version only)"
