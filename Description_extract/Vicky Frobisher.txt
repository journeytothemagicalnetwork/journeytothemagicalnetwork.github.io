"Vicky Frobisher was a witch who attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s and was sorted into Gryffindor house.In 1995, she tried out for the Gryffindor Quidditch team, but was denied the position of Keeper because she admitted she would put the Charms Club, to which she belonged, before Quidditch. Being unable to attend some practises was the reason Ron Weasley was picked, despite the fact that Vicky played better. According to Angelina, Vicky was also involved in other Hogwarts clubs and societies, besides Charms.Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (First mentioned)"
