"Professor Podric Piles was a wizard that was depicted in a large painting that hung in Gryffindor Tower, at Hogwarts Castle. In his lifetime, he was Head of Gryffindor House.In the painting, Piles is depicted as playing a game of Wizard's Chess with Uric Oapley, with Tobias Stump, standing, watching over the game, and Muldoon Cragg, in the background, absorbed in reading.
