"Nanette Desford was a witch and Ravenclaw student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. She was usually found spending time by the Clock Tower during the 19961997 school year. She often played Exploding Snap. She gave Harry Potter Chocolate Frog Cards whenever he beat her.This particular student was only featured in the console versions of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban and the Nintendo DS version of Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (First appearance) (Console versions)Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game) (NDS and PSP version only)"
