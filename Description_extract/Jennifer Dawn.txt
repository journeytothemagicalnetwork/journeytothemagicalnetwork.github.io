"Jennifer Dawn was a Gryffindor student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. She once signed up for Quidditch training at 7pm on Saturday on a sign-up sheet that was posted on the bulletin board in the Gryffindor Common Room.The name \"Jennifer\" comes from a Cornish form of the Welsh name Gwenhwyfar. This name has only been common outside of Cornwall since the beginning of the 20th century, after it was featured in George Bernard Shaw's play The Doctor's Dilemma (1906).\"Dawn\" is the time each morning at which daylight first begins.The Wizarding World of Harry Potter (Mentioned on a poster)"
