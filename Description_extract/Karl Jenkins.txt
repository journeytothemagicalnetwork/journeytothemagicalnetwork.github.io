"Karl Jenkins (b. 2005 or 2006) was a wizard and student in Albus Potter's year at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.Karl Jenkins was born somewhere in the United Kingdom between 1 September 2005 and 31 August 2006.Karl began attending Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry on 1 September 2017. As the sorting ceremony was starting, he expressed wonder at the prospect of Albus Potter, son of the famous Harry Potter, being in his year. Karl was sorted into Gryffindor house.When Albus Potter proved unable to get his broomstick to rise off the ground during their first flying lesson with Madam Hooch, Karl teased him, calling him the \"Slytherin Squib.\"During a second-year Potions lesson, Albus and Scorpius Malfoy had difficulty preparing their potion, prompting Karl to tell the other students in the class to \"leave [Albus] and Voldemorts child to it.\"Upon returning to 2020 after altering the past, Scorpius Malfoy found himself in an alternate reality in which Lord Voldemort won, and was greeted as the \"Scorpion King\" by Karl Jenkins on the Hogwarts grounds. Yann Fredericks asked Scorpius if he was still on for an event the following night and Karl enthused they were both ready to \"spill some proper Mudblood guts.\"In the original West End production of Harry Potter and the Cursed Child, Karl is played by Jack North.In the second West End cast (2017-2018) of Harry Potter and the Cursed Child, Karl is played by Tom Mackley.In the original Broadway production of Harry Potter and the Cursed Child, Karl is played by Joey LaBrasca.Harry Potter and the Cursed Child (First appearance)Harry Potter and the Cursed Child (play)Wizarding World (Mentioned only)"
