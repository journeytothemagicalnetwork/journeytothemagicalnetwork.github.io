"Curtis Evercreech was a wizard and Slytherin student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s.He was seen walking around the school during the 19931994 school year.Once during the 19961997 school year, he ate too many Cauldron Cakes, stating he felt so sick that he couldn't even look at those he had left. However, Harry Potter offered to take the Cauldron Cakes away for him, though he would not part with them unless he was given five Fanged Frisbees.\"Curtis\" is a personal name from an English surname which originally meant \"courteous\" in Old French.This particular student was only featured in the console versions of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban and the Nintendo DS version of Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (First appearance) (Console versions)Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game) (NDS version only)"
