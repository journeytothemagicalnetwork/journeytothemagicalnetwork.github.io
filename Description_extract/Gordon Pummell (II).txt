"Gordon Pummell was a Slytherin student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He blocked access to areas of the school grounds that were off limits during the 19961997 school year.The name \"Gordon\" comes from a Scottish surname which was originally derived from a place name meaning \"great hill\". It was originally used in honour of Charles George Gordon (1833-1885), a British general who died defending the city of Khartoum in Sudan.Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game) (Nintendo DS version only)"
