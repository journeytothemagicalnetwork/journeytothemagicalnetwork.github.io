"Emily was a witch and Gryffindor student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry during the 1990s. In 1992, she became a member of the Duelling Club and was ranked two on the Club's top-ten duellists list.The name \"Emily\" is an English feminine form of Aemilius. In the English-speaking world it was not common until after the German House of Hanover came to the British throne in the 18th century; the princess Amelia Sophia (1711-1786) was commonly known as Emily in English, even though Amelia is an unrelated name.
