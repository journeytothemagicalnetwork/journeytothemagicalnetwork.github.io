"Riley Frazer was a Gryffindor student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Riley once signed up for Quidditch training at 7pm on Saturday on a sign-up sheet that was posted on the bulletin board in the Gryffindor Common Room.The name \"Riley\" comes from a surname which is either a variant of Reilly or is derived from a place name meaning \"rye clearing\" in Old English.The Wizarding World of Harry Potter (Mentioned on a poster)"
