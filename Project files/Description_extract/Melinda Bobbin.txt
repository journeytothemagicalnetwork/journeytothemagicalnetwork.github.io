"Melinda Bobbin was a witch who attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry during the 1990s. Horace Slughorn described her as \"charming\" and made her a member of the Slug Club in 1996. Her family owned a large chain of apothecaries.A \"bobbin\" is a spool or reel that holds thread or yarn for spinning, weaving, knitting, sewing, or making lace.Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (First mentioned)"
