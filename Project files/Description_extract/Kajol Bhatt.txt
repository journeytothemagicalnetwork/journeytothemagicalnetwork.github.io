"Kajol Bhatt was a witch and Gryffindor student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. She was seen walking around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.Kajol is a female Indian name, which means \"eye liner\" or \"mascara\". Bhatt is an Indian surname meaning \"priest\".Based on her name, it can be presumed that she is of Indian descent.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (First appearance)"
