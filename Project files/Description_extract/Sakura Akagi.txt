"Sakura Akagi was a Gryffindor student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. She was seen walking around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.Sakura is a Japanese given name, written as . It means \"cherry blossom\". It can also be written as  (saku) \"blossom\",  (ra) \"good\". Akagi is likewise Japanese and means either \"red castle, \"red tree\", \"crimson\" or \"red future\".Given her name, she is most likely of Japanese descent.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (PS2 version only)"
