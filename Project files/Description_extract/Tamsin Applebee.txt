"Tamsin Applebee was a witch who attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, and was a member of Hufflepuff house. She played as a Chaser on the Hufflepuff Quidditch team when it was captained by Cedric Diggory.Tamsin is a contracted form of Thomasina. It was traditionally used in Cornwall.Harry Potter: Quidditch World Cup (First appearance)"
