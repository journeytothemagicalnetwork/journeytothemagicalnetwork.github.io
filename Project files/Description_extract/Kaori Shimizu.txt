"Kaori Shimizu (  Shimizu Kaori) was a witch and Gryffindor student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s who was seen walking around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.The name Kaori means \"fragrance, fragrant\" () (ka) and \"weave, weaving\" () (ori).Kaori's surname Shimizu means \"clear, pure, clean\" () (shi) and \"water\" () (mizu).Based on her name, she is most likely of Japanese descent.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (First appearance) (Console versions)"
