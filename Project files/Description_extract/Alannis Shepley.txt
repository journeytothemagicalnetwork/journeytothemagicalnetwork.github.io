"Alannis Shepley was a witch and Ravenclaw student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. She played casual Quidditch matches with other students during the 19961997 school year.She may the same person as the Alannis who appeared in the PC and console version of the Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince as Captain of the Ravenclaw Duelling Club.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (PS2 version only)Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game) (NDS version only)"
