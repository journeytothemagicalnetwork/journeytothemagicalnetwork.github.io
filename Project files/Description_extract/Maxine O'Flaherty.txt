"Maxine O'Flaherty was a witch that attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s.She was sorted sometime before 1994 into Hufflepuff. She was one of the Beaters for the Hufflepuff Quidditch team at the time that it was captained by Cedric Diggory.Harry Potter: Quidditch World Cup (First appearance)"
