"Terry Skeres was a wizard and Gryffindor student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry during the 1990s.On May 2 1998, he participated in the Battle of Hogwarts, helping defend the school against Death Eaters. It is unknown if he survived the battle, but he possibly did, as his name was not mentioned among the Fallen Fifty. Terry Skeres was portrayed in Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2 by Freddie Hogan. Hogan was not credited in the film, nor was his character's name given as \"Terry Skeres\" in any official source, but this name is listed on Hogan's casting agency profile page.Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2"
