"Thomas McGruder was a wizard and Slytherin student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s.During the 19961997 school year, he blocked the door at one end of the Stone Bridge, forcing Harry Potter to duel with him in order to get past.The name \"Thomas\" is the Greek form of the Aramaic name  (Ta'oma') which meant \"twin\".Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (First appearance) (PS2 version only)Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game) (NDS version only)"
