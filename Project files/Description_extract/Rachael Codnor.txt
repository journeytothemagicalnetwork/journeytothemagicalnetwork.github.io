"Rachael Codnor was a Gryffindor student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s.During the 19961997 school year, she was usually seen in the Paved Courtyard, challenging other students to games of Wizard Skittles.\"Rachael\" is from the Hebrew name  (Rachel) meaning \"ewe\".This particular student was only featured in the PlayStation 2 version of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban and the Nintendo DS version of Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (PS2 version only)Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game) (NDS version only)"
