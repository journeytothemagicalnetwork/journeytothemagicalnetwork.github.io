"Nigel Wroxton was a wizard and Ravenclaw student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He competed in the school Gobstones tournament during the 19961997 school year.The name \"Nigel\" comes from Nigellus, a medieval Latinized form of NEIL. It was commonly associated with Latin niger \"black\". It was revived in the 19th century, perhaps in part due to Sir Walter Scott's novel The Fortunes of Nigel (1822).Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (First appearance) (Console version)Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game) (NDS version only)"
