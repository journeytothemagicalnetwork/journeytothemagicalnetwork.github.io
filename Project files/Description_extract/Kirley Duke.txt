"Kirley Duke McCormack (b. 1971) was wizard from Great Britain or Ireland and the lead guitarist for the popular band The Weird Sisters, one of its first three members. Kirley was the son of Quidditch player Catriona McCormack and the brother of Meaghan McCormack. Both women played for the Pride of Portree. He attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry from 1983.Kirley appeared to be a man with few words.Guitar: Kirley owned a guitar, which was confiscated by Argus Filch in the 19891990 school year, which was retrieved by Jacob's sibling and returned to him. He used this guitar to play at numerous events, such as the concert at the Great Lake, the party at the Three Broomsticks Inn and the Yule Ball.A \"Duke\" is a nobleman with the highest hereditary rank, especially a man of the highest grade of the peerage in Great Britain.Kirley Duke was portrayed by Jonny Greenwood of Radiohead in the film adaptation of Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire.Kirley's appearance as depicted in the video games differs.
