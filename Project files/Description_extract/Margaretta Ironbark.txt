"Margaretta Ironbark was a witch who founded the Dragon Club at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry during the 1800s.In her youth, she received her magical education from Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. She was in a study group with Olin Oakwood and Skylar Flame. She was best friends with Olin. She and Flame pressured Olin to compete in the tournament with them. When Oakwood defeated them and won the Tournament, their study group fell apart and each of them formed their own clubs, each one dedicated to the areas of study that was best suited for them.Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery (First appearance) (Appears in portrait(s))"
