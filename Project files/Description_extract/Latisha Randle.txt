"Latisha Randle was a witch and Ravenclaw student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s.In the 19961997 school year, she took a pair of shoes that belonged to Luna Lovegood, with the intent of hiding them as a prank. Luna wanted to wear these shoes to Professor Slughorn's Christmas party as part of her outfit. Harry Potter had to give her an Oculus Potion in order to get her to return the shoes.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (First appearance) (Console versions)Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game) (NDS version only)"
