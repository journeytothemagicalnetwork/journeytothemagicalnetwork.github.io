"Victor Ketsueki was a British or Irish wizard who attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry from 1984 to 1991 and was Sorted into Ravenclaw.Victor began attending Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in 1984 and was Sorted into Ravenclaw. Victor talking about his experience During the summer of 1990, he was allegedly turned into a vampire, and so gained some fame in the next school year. According to himself, he attended all-night parties in the catacombs beneath Paris and danced with Veela until dawn. At some point during the 19901991 school year, he was seen talking about his experience with a bunch of students in the Great Hall.Victor helped Jacob's sibling and Chiara Lobosca clear out the doxy infestation in Hogwarts Castle, in particular in the Hogwarts kitchens.Charms: Victor was proficient with the Knockback Jinx and Shield Charm, which he used to help deal with an infestation of doxies.\"Ketsueki\" (written in kanji as ) is the Japanese word for blood, which is most likely referential of his supposed vampirism.Based on his surname, it is possible that he was of Japanese descent.It is unknown if he ever did become an actual vampire, or whether or not he fabricated the tale for attention. It is also unknown if, or how, humans can be turned into vampires in the Harry Potter universe.Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery (First appearance)"
