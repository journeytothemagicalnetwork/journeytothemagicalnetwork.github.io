"James, first name unknown, was a wizard who attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s and was Sorted into Hufflepuff. By 1993, he was a member of the Hufflepuff Quidditch team, playing in an unknown position.James only appeared on a piece of concept artwork for the film adaptation of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban, that can be seen in the book Harry Potter: The Character Vault.The name \"James\" can be read on this individual's Quidditch robes, meaning that this is the character's surname, seeing as other uniform concept arts and those seen in the actual films feature surnames only (e.g. \"Potter\" and \"Zabini\").Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (film) (First appearance) (Concept artwork)Harry Potter: The Character Vault (Concept artwork)"
