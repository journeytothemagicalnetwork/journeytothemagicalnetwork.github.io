"Shoma Ichikawa was a wizard and Ravenclaw student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He was a member of the Duelling Club run by Helen Dawlish in the Transfiguration Courtyard. He gave Harry Potter some Honeywater so that he could make Wiggenweld Potion.This particular student was only featured in the console version of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban and the Nintendo DS version of Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (First appearance)Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game) (NDS version only)"
