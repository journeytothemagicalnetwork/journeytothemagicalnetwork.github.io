"Neil Randall was a Gryffindor student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He was a member of the Duelling Club run by Karl Limpley in the Clock Tower Courtyard during the 19961997 school year.The name \"Neil\" comes from the Gaelic name Niall, which is of disputed origin, possibly meaning \"champion\" or \"cloud\". This was the name of a semi-legendary 4th-century Irish king, Niall of the Nine Hostages.This particular student was only featured in the PlayStation 2 version of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban and the Nintendo DS version of Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (PS2 version only)Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game) (NDS version only)"
