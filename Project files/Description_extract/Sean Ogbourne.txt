"Sean Ogbourne was a wizard and Gryffindor student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He was usually seen spending time by the entrance to the Quidditch pitch during the 19961997 school year.This particular student was only featured in the console versions of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban and the Nintendo DS version of Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (First appearance) (Console versions)Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game) (NDS version only)"
