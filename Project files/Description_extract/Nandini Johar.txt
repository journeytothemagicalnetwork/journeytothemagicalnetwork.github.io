"Nandini Johar was a witch Gryffindor student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s.During the 19961997 school year at Hogwarts, she was usually seen spending time by the Herbology Greenhouses. She confessed to Harry Potter some time that school year that she would have liked a new broom. She also admitted to being a \"disaster\" in Potions.Nandini is an Indian surname, and can either mean \"daughter\", \"one who brings joy\", \"Ganga\" or \"Goddess Durga\". Johar is a surnme of Indian origin.This particular student was only featured in the console version of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban and the Nintendo DS version of Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince.Given her name, she is most likely of Indian descent.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (First appearance) (Console versions only)Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game) (NDS version only)"
