"The Slytherin Wizard's Chess champion, as the title suggests, refers to the Wizard's Chess Champion for Slytherin House, at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.The boy came to be educated at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and was Sorted into Slytherin House. His Head of House was Professor Severus Snape, the Potions Master, and he shared a dormitory with four other boys in his year in the Slytherin Dungeon.
