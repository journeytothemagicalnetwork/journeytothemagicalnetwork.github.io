"Professor Tobias Stump was a wizard that was depicted in a large painting that hung in Gryffindor Tower, at Hogwarts Castle. In his lifetime, he was Head of Gryffindor House.In the painting, Stump is depicted as watching over a game of Wizard's Chess between Podric Piles and Uric Oapley, with Muldoon Cragg standing behind him, absorbed in reading.
