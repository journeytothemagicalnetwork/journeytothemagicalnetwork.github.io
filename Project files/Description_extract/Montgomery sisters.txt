"The Montgomery sisters were two witches who attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in 1997.Their five-year old brother was bitten by the savage werewolf Fenrir Greyback when their mother refused to help the Death Eaters. Their brother later died at St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries, because the Healers there were unable to save his life, given the extent of his injuries.Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (First mentioned)"
