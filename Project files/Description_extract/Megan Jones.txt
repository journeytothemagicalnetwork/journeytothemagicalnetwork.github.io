"Megan Jones was a half-blood witch who began attending Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in 1991. She was in the same year as Harry Potter and was Sorted into Hufflepuff house.Megan was born in either 1979 or 1980. She may be related to Hestia, Peter and Gwenog Jones.Megan began attending Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in September 1991.She would have boarded the Hogwarts Express and arrived at Hogsmeade Station to the school. Along with the rest of the first years, she would have boarded an enchanted boat and be welcomed by Minerva McGonagall in the Entrance Hall Antechamber.They proceeded to the Great Hall where the Sorting ceremony was held. The Sorting Hat placed Megan in Hufflepuff House.
