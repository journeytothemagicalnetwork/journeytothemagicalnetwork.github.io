"Morag (c. 1981) was a witch and a Hogwarts student in her youth. She was sorted into either Ravenclaw, Slytherin or Hufflepuff on 1 September, 1992.Sometime in late 1992, during a Transfiguration class about Transfiguring a matchstick Morag tried to light hers, and was scolded for it by Professor McGonagall.It is possible that Morag is Morag MacDougal, a student in Harry Potter's year. She may have repeated her first year.Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (video game) (First appearance)"
