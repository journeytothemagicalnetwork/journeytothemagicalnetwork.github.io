"Kris Kolumbiko (b. 1730s/1740s) was a Slytherin student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1750s. He was Captain of the Slytherin Quidditch team from 1752 to 1756. Kris Kolumbiko's name appearing on a plaque The naming seems to be a variant spelling of Chris Columbus, director of the first two Harry Potter films and a producer of the third.Harry Potter films (Name appears on plaque)"
