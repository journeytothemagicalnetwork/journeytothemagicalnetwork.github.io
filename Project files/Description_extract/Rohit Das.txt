"Rohit Das was a Hufflepuff student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He was seen walking around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.The name Rohit is Sanskrit and means \"red\" or \"first rays of the sun\". Das is an Indian surname, meaning \"servant of God\".Given his name, he is most likely of Indian descent.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (PS2 version only)"
