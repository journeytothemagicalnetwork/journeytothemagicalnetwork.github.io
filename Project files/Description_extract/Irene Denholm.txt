"Irene Denholm was a witch and Hufflepuff student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. During the 19961997 school year, she could often be found in the First-floor corridor, as well as played Exploding Snap with anyone who was willing.The name \"Irene\" is from Greek  (Eirene), derived from a word meaning \"peace\". This was the name of the Ancient Greek goddess who personified peace, one of the  (Horai). It was also borne by several early Christian saints. The name was common in the Byzantine Empire, notably being borne by an 8th-century Empress, Eirene Sarantapechaina (reigned 797 - 802) who was the first woman to lead the Empire.
