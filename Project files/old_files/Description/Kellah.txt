[[1980]])<ref name=\"age\" /> was a [[Wizardkind|witch]] who attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] from [[1991]] to [[1998]] and was [[Sorting ceremony|Sorted]] into [[Gryffindor]] [[Hogwarts Houses|House]].<ref>[http://www.yptc.co.uk/agency/kandicemorris.php Kandice Morris's profile] from the Young Persons Theatre Group Casting Agency</ref><ref name=\"POAF\"/>\n\nKellah shared a dormitory in [[Gryffindor Tower]] with [[Hermione Granger]], [[Lavender Brown]], [[Parvati Patil]], and [[Unidentified red-haired Gryffindor girl|another girl]] whom she stayed with for six years. In her [[third year]] at Hogwarts, she chose [[Care of Magical Creatures]] as an elective subject, with [[Rubeus Hagrid]] as her [[professor]].<ref name=\"POAF\">{{POA|F}}</ref>\n\n==Biography==\n===Early life===\nKellah was born in either [[1979]] or [[1980]],<ref name=\"age\"/> likely in either [[Great Britain]] or [[Ireland]].\n\n===Hogwarts years===\n====Early years====\nIn [[1991]], Kellah began her magical studies at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] and was [[Sorting ceremony|Sorted]] into [[Gryffindor|Gryffindor House]], home to the brave and daring, during the [[Sorting ceremony]]. Along with the other Gryffindors, Kellah was led by [[Percy Weasley]] to the [[Gryffindor Tower|Gryffindor Common Room]] and through the [[Fat Lady]]'s portrait. She shared a dormitory with [[Hermione Granger]], [[Lavender Brown]], [[Parvati Patil]] and other unidentified girls. Her [[Head of House]] was [[Minerva McGonagall]], the [[Professor]] of [[Transfiguration (class)|Transfiguration]].<ref name=\"POAF\"/>\n\n====Third year====\n[[File:Kellahpoa.PNG|150px|thumb|left|Kellah sitting with Harry, Ron, and Hermione during the Welcoming Feast]]\n\nIn [[1993]], Kellah did clap after [[Rubeus Hagrid]] was announced as [[Silvanus Kettleburn]]'s successor as the [[Care of Magical Creatures]] [[professor]], although very unenthused, unlike her fellow enthusiastic Gryffindors, implying she may not have been massively fond of him.<ref name=\"POAF\"/>\n\n[[File:SeamusKellah.PNG|150px|thumb|Kellah sitting next to [[Seamus Finnigan]] during a DADA lesson]]\n\n[[Remus Lupin]] was appointed as the [[Defence Against the Dark Arts]] [[professor]] that school year. Kellah attended her first Defence Against the Dark Arts class when she presented a [[Boggart]] to the class.<ref name=\"POAF\"/> One by one, each student had to confront the Boggart, which would take the form of their greatest fear. Kellah's turned into either a [[rat]], [[rattlesnake]] or a bloody eyeball.<ref name=\"POA7\"/>\n\n[[File:Ron kellahbroom.PNG|150px|thumb|left|Kellah with Ron presenting Harry's broken broom]]\n\nThis year, she chose [[Care of Magical Creatures]] as an elective subject and was present when [[Professor]] [[Rubeus Hagrid]], [[Professor]] [[Silvanus Kettleburn]]'s successor, presented a [[Hippogriff]] named [[Buckbeak]] to the class. Kellah also was around Harry Potter's bed in the hospital wing and presented his broken broomstick to him with [[Ronald Weasley|Ron Weasley]]. Kellah appeared to be close with [[Seamus Finnigan]], opting to sit with him during lessons and meal times as opposed to her friends [[Lavender Brown]] and [[Parvati Patil]].<ref name=\"POAF\"/>\n\n==Physical appearance==\nShe was a black girl with black/brown hair worn in braids. She wore a single stud earring on each ear.<ref name=\"POAF\"/>\n\n==Behind the scenes==\n*Kellah appears only in {{POA|FA}}.
