was born somewhere in the [[Great Britain|United Kingdom]] between [[1 September]] [[2006|2005]] and [[31 August]] [[2007|2006]].<ref name=\"act1scene4\">{{CC|B|1|4}}</ref>\n\n===First year===\nYann began attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] on 1 September [[2017]] and was sorted into [[Gryffindor]].<ref name=\"act1scene4\" /><ref name=\"act2scene11\">{{CC|B|2|11}}</ref> During the [[sorting ceremony]], he remarked that [[Albus Potter]] had hair just like his father, [[Harry Potter]].<ref name=\"act1scene4\" /> He sardonically retracted this comment when Albus was placed in [[Slytherin]] a short time later.<ref name=\"act1scene4\" />\n\nYann was able to raise his [[broomstick]] on the first attempt during the [[first year]]s' initial [[Flying (class)|flying]] lesson with [[Rolanda Hooch|Madam Hooch]].<ref name=\"act1scene4\" /> The only other student in the class who accomplished this feat was [[Rose Granger-Weasley]].<ref name=\"act1scene4\" />\n\n===Fourth year===\nUpon returning to [[2020]] after [[Time-Turner|altering the past]], Albus Potter and [[Scorpius Malfoy]] found themselves in an [[alternate reality]], in which Albus was a Gryffindor and [[Hermione Granger]] taught [[Defence Against the Dark Arts]].<ref name=\"act2scene11\" /> During a lesson, Albus questioned her about the changed circumstances, prompting her to deduct points from Gryffindor.<ref name=\"act2scene11\" /> Albus persisted, however, prompting Yann to threateningly insinuate that he would do something to Albus if he didn't stop.<ref name=\"act2scene11\" />\n\nAfter finding himself in another alternate reality, in which [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]] won and Albus was never born, Scorpius was greeted by Yann and [[Karl Jenkins]] in the Hogwarts grounds.<ref name=\"act3scene2\">{{CC|B|3|2}}</ref> Yann asked Scorpius if he was still on for an event the following night and Karl enthused they were both ready to \"spill some proper [[Muggle-born|Mudblood]] guts.\"<ref name=\"act3scene2\" />\n\n==Magical abilities and skills==\n*'''[[Flight|Flying]]''': Judging by his performance in his first [[Flying (class)|Flying]] lesson at
