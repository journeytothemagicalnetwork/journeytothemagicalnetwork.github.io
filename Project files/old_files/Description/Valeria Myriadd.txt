After she died, numerous [[portrait]]s were hung of her at [[Hogwarts Castle]].\n\n==Biography==\n===Life===\nValeria was born someplace in either [[Great Britain]] or [[Ireland]],<ref name=\"PLACE\">\"Hogwarts just serves Britain and Ireland\" from the South West News Service on [[8 July]], [[2000]] (transcript available [http://www.accio-quote.org/articles/2000/0700-swns-alfie.htm here])</ref> and when she was eleven years old began attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]], where she was sorted into [[Gryffindor]] [[Hogwarts Houses|House]].\nShe graduated at the end of her [[seventh year]] with good enough grades that she could return as a [[professor]],<ref name=\"TMOHP\"/> and eventually rose to the position of [[Head of Gryffindor House]].<ref name=\"TMOHP\"/> She died sometime prior to [[September]] [[1991]].<ref name=\"DEATH\"/>\n\n===[[1991\u20131992 school year]]===\nBy [[1991]], several portraits of Valeria hung at the main halls of [[Hogwarts Castle]], such as the [[Grand Staircase]]. In [[Gryffindor Tower]], one of her portraits guarded a secret passage to a [[Disused Bathroom (Fred and George's Shop)|disused bathroom]]. The password for said passage was \"''Pig Snout''\".<ref>{{PS|G}} - PS2 version</ref>\n\n===[[1992-1993 school year]]===\nBy the [[1992-1993 school year|next school year]], her place in the Gryffindor Tower had been taken by [[Oraclitus Spheer]]. Her portrait was moved to the [[Hogwarts Library]] Annexe, hiding a shortcut to a section containing the original hand-written manuscript of ''[[Hogwarts: A History]]'', by [[Chroniculus Punnet]].
