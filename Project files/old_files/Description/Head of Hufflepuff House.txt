It is unknown how a professor would be elected for the post, but it's most likely that the headmaster chooses the professor who embodies all the qualities [[Helga Hufflepuff]] herself had (loyalty, kindness, generosity, etc.) and probably was in Hufflepuff House when he/she was in Hogwarts.<ref name=\"PS7\">{{PS|B|7}}</ref><ref
