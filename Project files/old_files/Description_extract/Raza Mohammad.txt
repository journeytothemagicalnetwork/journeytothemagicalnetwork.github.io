"Raza Mohammad was a Gryffindor student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s.During the 19961997 school year, he was usually seen in the Clock Tower Courtyard, where he enjoyed playing Gobstones with other students.Raza is an Arabic male name derived from Ridha, meaning \"satisfaction, contentment\".This particular student was only featured in the PlayStation 2 version of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban and the Nintendo DS version of Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (PS2 version only)Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game) (NDS version only)"
