"Bhupen Shastri was a Slytherin student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He was seen walking around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.Bhupen is an Indian given name, of Sanskrit origin, meaning \"king.\" Shastri is a surname of Indian origin. Given his name, it can be presumed the he is of Indian descent.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (PS2 version only)"
