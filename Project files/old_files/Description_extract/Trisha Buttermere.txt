"Trisha Buttermere was a Hufflepuff student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. She was extremely fond of Butterbeer and kept a large supply of it at Hogwarts, which allowed her to trade bottles of the beverage for chess pieces, Chocolate Frog Cards, and Gobstones during the 19961997 school year.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (PS2 version only)Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game) (NDS version only)"
