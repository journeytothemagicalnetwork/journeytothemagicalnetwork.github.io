"Swati Pevekar was a Ravenclaw student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. She was seen walking around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.Swati is the Indian name of the third brightest star in the night sky, the same as Arcturus in the western world.This student was only featured in the PlayStation 2 version of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban.Based on her name, it can be presumed that she is of Indian descent.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (PS2 version only)"
