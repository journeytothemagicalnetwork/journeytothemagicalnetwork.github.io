"Kousuke Yoshioka was a Hufflepuff student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He was seen walking around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.Kousuke is a Japanese name meaning \"inlet\" and \"clear\". Yoshioka is a Japanese surname, which means \"lucky (or good) hill\".Based on his name, he is most likely of Japanese descent.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (PS2 version only)"
