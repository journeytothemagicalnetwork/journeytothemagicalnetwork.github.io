"Colby Frey was a wizard who started attending Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in 2008 and was a member of Slytherin house. He was the younger twin brother of Fischer Frey.At the age of eleven, he and Fischer began attending Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in Slytherin house. They befriended Cassandra Vole, and she led their group in bullying their fellow students including Daniel Page.They went to the Library one night to find a newspaper article about Daniel. Daniel, Ivy Warrington and their friend found out that they had broken curfew and went to find out why. They watched the group at a distance, until Colby noticed and told Cassandra.Colby had a mean-spirited personality and enjoyed bullying others with his friends. He was known to enjoy Herbology, handcraft and poetry, and he disliked History of Magic which he was not good at.Charms: Colby was capable of producing a corporeal Patronus in the form of a rhinoceros, an exceptional magical feat for a Hogwarts student.Herbology: Colby was known to enjoy the subject of Herbology at Hogwarts, the study of magical plants and fungi.Wand: Colby's wand was 12 inches, made of ash with a unicorn hair core.Harry Potter: Magic Awakened (First appearance)"
