"Hortense Cooper was a witch and Ravenclaw student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. She told Harry Potter that she could use the Reductor Curse on suits of armour in order to find Stink Pellets during the 19961997 school year.\"Hortense\" is a feminine form of the Roman family name Hortensius, possibly derived from Latin hortus \"garden\".A \"cooper\" is one whose occupation is the making of barrels, tubs, and other vessels formed of staves and hoops.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (First appearance) (Console versions only)Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game) (NDS version only)"
