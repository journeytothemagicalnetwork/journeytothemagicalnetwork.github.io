"Sourav Kartik was a Slytherin student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He was seen walking around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.Sourav is a common male Indian name, meaning \"fragrance\", generally that of flowers. Kartik, a male given name, is commonly used in the southern part of India. The name stands for the Hindu god \"Karthikeya\", a son of Shiva.This student was only featured in the PlayStation 2 version of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban.Based on his name, it can be presumed that he is of Indian descent.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (PS2 version only)"
