"Takashi Noda was a wizard and Ravenclaw student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He was seen walking around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.Takashi is a Japanese masculine given name, which can have several meanings, depending on which Kanjis one uses to write the name with. Noda is a Japanese surname, meaning \"rice paddy in the field\":Given his name, it can be presumed that he is of Japanese descent.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (First appearance) (Console versions)"
