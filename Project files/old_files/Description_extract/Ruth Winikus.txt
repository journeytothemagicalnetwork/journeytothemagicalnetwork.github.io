"Ruth Winikus was a Gryffindor student who attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s.Ruth signed up to join the Hogwarts Gobstone Club on a sign-up sheet that was posted on the bulletin board in Gryffindor Tower. She also registered for a trip to Hogsmeade on another sign-up sheet posted on the same bulletin board, which was scheduled for Saturday the 15th in an unknown month.This character is a tribute to Ruth Winick, who worked as a graphic artist on Philosopher's Stone and Chamber of Secrets, and as a draughtsman on Prisoner of Azkaban and Goblet of Fire.Harry Potter films (Mentioned on a poster)"
