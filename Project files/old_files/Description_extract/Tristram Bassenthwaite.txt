"Tristram Bassenthwaite was a Slytherin student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He was seen walking around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.Tristram is a Medieval English form of Tristan. Bassenthwaite is a real village in the Lake District. Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (PS2 version only)"
