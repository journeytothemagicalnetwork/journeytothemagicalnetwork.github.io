"Andrew Snowy Owl was a Gryffindor student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s.Andrew added his name to a sign-up sheet for the Hogwarts Gobstone Club that was posted on the bulletin board in Gryffindor Tower. He also registered for a trip to Hogsmeade on another sign-up sheet posted on the same bulletin board, which was scheduled for Saturday the 15th in an unknown month.This character is a presumably a tribute to Andrew Ackland-Snow, who worked as an art director on all eight Harry Potter films.Harry Potter films (Mentioned on a poster)"
