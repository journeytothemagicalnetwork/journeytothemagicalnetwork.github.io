"Raymond was a wizard and Ravenclaw student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.Raymond was born between 1974 and 1980, if not earlier. He was accepted into Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and was sorted into Ravenclaw House.Harry Potter first met Raymond in the Hogwarts Grounds, in 1991. The boy was sitting next to a well and when Harry went closer, he told him that his kitten fell into the well. Later, Harry was in a potions side-room and a cloaked Quirinus Quirrell blasted him in a potions storeroom with the Deprimo charm. After defeating some Trolls Harry went into the bottom of the well and saw Raymond on top. That was the first time he mentioned his name to Harry. After giving back his kitten, Raymond threw a Chocolate Frog Card to Harry.The name \"Raymond\" comes From the Germanic name Raginmund, composed of the elements ragin \"advice\" and mund \"protector\".Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (video game) (First appearance) (PS1 version only)"
