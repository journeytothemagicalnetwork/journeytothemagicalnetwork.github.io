"Hardik Tandel was a wizard and Gryffindor student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He was seen walking around during the 1993-1994 school year.Hardik is an Indian male given name, meaning \"heartfelt\". Tandel is a surname of Indian origin, widely found in the Gujarat state.This student was only featured in the console version of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban.Given his name, it can be presumed that he is of Indian descent.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (First appearance) (Console versions)"
