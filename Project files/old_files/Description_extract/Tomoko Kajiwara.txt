"Tomoko Kajiwara was a Ravenclaw student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. She was seen walking around the school during the 19931994 school year.\"Tomoko\" is a Japanese name,  (tomo) means \"wisdom, intellect\" or  (tomo) \"friend\", combined with the popular suffix  (ko), which means \"child\".Based on her name, she is most likely of Japanese descent.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (PS2 version only)"
