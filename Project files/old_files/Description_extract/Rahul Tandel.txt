"Rahul Tandel was a wizard and Gryffindor student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He was seen walking around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.Rahul is an Indian name, meaning \"capable\". Rahul was the name of the son of Buddha. Tandel is a surname of Indian origin, widely found in the Gujarat state.This student was only featured in the PlayStation 2 version of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban.Based on his name, it can be presumed that he is of Indian descent.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (First appearance) (Console versions)"
