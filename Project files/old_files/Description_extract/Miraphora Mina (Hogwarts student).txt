"Miraphora Mina was a witch and Gryffindor student who attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. She signed up to join the Hogwarts Gobstone Club on a sign-up sheet that was posted on the bulletin board in Gryffindor Tower.This character was named after Miraphora Mina, a graphic artist who worked on every Harry Potter film from Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone onward.Harry Potter films (First mentioned) (Mentioned on a poster)"
