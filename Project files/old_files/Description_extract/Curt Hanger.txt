"Curt Hanger was a wizard and Gryffindor student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s.Curt added his name to a sign-up sheet for the Hogwarts Gobstone Club that was posted on the bulletin board in Gryffindor Tower. He also registered for a trip to Hogsmeade on another sign-up sheet posted on the Gryffindor bulletin board, which was scheduled for Saturday the 15th in an unknown month.This character's name is a play on the word \"coathanger.\"Harry Potter films (Mentioned on a poster)Harry Potter: The Exhibition (Mentioned on a poster)"
