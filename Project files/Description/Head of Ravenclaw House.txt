The Head of House also served as a Professor of a subject, presumably an academic subject for a Head of Ravenclaw. Most, if not all, of the heads of Ravenclaw house attended Hogwarts and were [[Sorting ceremony|sorted]] into Ravenclaw during their attendance at the school, such as [[Filius Flitwick]]. It is unknown how a professor would be elected to the post, but it was possible that the [[headmaster]] chose a professor who embodied the qualities of house founder [[Rowena Ravenclaw]], such as intelligence, wisdom, and wit.<ref name=\"PS7\">{{PS|B|7}}</ref><ref name=\"OOTP11\">{{OOTP|B|11}}</ref>\n\nThe first Head of Ravenclaw House was the famous co-founder of Hogwarts, Rowena Ravenclaw;<ref name=\"COS9\"/> the only other known Head of House was [[Filius Flitwick]].<ref name=\"HBP29\"/>\n\n==Known Heads of Ravenclaw==\n*[[Rowena Ravenclaw]] - One of the four [[Hogwarts founders]] and founder of the [[Ravenclaw|Ravenclaw House]].<ref name=\"COS9\"/>\n*[[Filius Flitwick]] - Head of Ravenclaw during [[Harry Potter]]'s time at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]].<ref name=\"HBP29\">{{HBP|B|29}}</ref>\n\n==See also==\n*[[Head of Gryffindor House]]\n*[[Head of Hufflepuff House]]\n*[[Head of Slytherin House]]\n*[[Ravenclaw]]\n*[[Professor]]\n*[[Hogwarts subjects]]\n*[[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]]\n\n==Appearances==\n*{{PS}} {{1st}}\n*{{PS|F}}\n*{{PS|G}}\n*{{COS}}\n*{{COS|F}}\n*{{COS|G}}\n*{{POA}}\n*{{POA|F}} \n*{{POA|G}}\n*{{GOF}}\n*{{GOF|F}}\n*{{OOTP}}\n*{{OOTP|F}}\n*{{OOTP|G}}\n*{{HBP}}\n*{{HBP|F}}\n*{{HBP|G}}\n*{{DH}}\n*{{DH|F2}}\n*{{DH|G2}}\n*{{LEGO|B}}\n*{{LEGO|C}}\n*{{QWC}}\n*{{LEGO|Y1}}\n*{{LEGO|Y2}}\n*{{HPK}}\n*{{LEGO}}\n*{{PM}} \n*{{WW}} \n*{{TCG}}\n*{{MHP}}\n*{{HPV|CH}}\n*{{HPV|CR}} {{Mention}}\n*{{HM}}\n*{{WU}}\n*{{PAS}}\n\n==Notes and references==\n{{Reflist}}\n[[es:Jefe de la Casa Ravenclaw]]\n[[pl:Opiekun Ravenclawu]]\n[[ru:\u0414\u0435\u043a\u0430\u043d \u0444\u0430\u043a\u0443\u043b\u044c\u0442\u0435\u0442\u0430 \u041a\u043e\u0433\u0442\u0435\u0432\u0440\u0430\u043d]]\n[[Category:Heads of House]]\n[[Category:Hogwarts positions]]\n[[Category:Ravenclaws]]"
