[[1972]]) was a [[Muggle-born]] [[Wizardkind|wizard]],<ref name=\"fwc\"/> who played [[bass]] for the popular wizarding band [[Weird Sisters|The Weird Sisters]].<ref name=\"COSG\"/>\n\n==Biography==\n===Early life===\nDonaghan Tremlett was born in [[1972]] to [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] [[Tremlett family|parents]].<ref name=\"fwc\" /> He started playing the bass guitar at some point in his childhood, and was noted to be talented at it during his Hogwarts years.<ref name=\"HM2WS\">{{HM|2|The Weird Sisters!}}</ref>\n\nDonaghan began attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in [[1983]] or [[1984]].<ref>[http://harrypotter.tokenzone.com/set_2001/tokens/token_11.swf Flash card] from Harry Potter Magical Trading Cards</ref> During his Hogwarts years, he saw the [[Weird Sisters|The Weird Sisters]] perform their song ''[[Do the Hippogriff]]'' and auditioned to become the new bass player of the band. Donaghan became a member of the band, replacing [[Jacob's sibling]], as he could actually play the bass without the use of [[magic]].<ref name=\"HM2WS\"/>\n\nDonaghan's favourite [[Quidditch]] [[Quidditch team|team]] was the [[Kenmare Kestrels]].<ref name=\"fwc\" />\n\n===Later life===\n[[File:Donaghan Tremlett playing bass.png|thumb|left|140px|Donaghan Tremlett playing at the Yule Ball]]\nOn [[25 December|Christmas Day]] in [[1994]], the band attended the [[Yule Ball]] at Hogwarts,<ref name=\"GOF23\">{{GOF|B|23}}</ref> where they played classic hits like \"[[Magic Works]]\" and \"[[This Is the Night]]\", as well as a new song titled \"[[Do the Hippogriff]]\".<ref name=\"GOFF\">{{GOF|F}}</ref>\n\nDonaghan married [[Donaghan Tremlett's spouse|an individual]] in September [[1995]]. The wedding was mentioned in the ''[[Daily Prophet]]'' on [[7 September]].<ref name=\"OOTP14\">{{OOTP|B|14}} - \"'No,' she sighed, 'just some guff about the bass player in the Weird Sisters getting married. ...'\"</ref>\n\n[[File:Funeral of Albus Dumbledore.png|250px|thumb|right|The [[Funeral of Albus Dumbledore]] that Donaghan attended]]\nIn June [[1997]], Donaghan attended the [[Funeral of Albus Dumbledore|funeral]] of [[Albus Dumbledore]], and was one of several familiar people [[Harry Potter]] noticed among the crowd.<ref name=\"hbp30\">{{HBP|B|30}}</ref>\n\nAfter the [[British Ministry of Magic|Ministry of Magic]] was [[Fall of the Ministry of Magic|taken over]] by [[Tom Riddle|Lord Voldemort]] in September 1997, Donaghan, as a [[Muggle-born]], presumably faced charges before the [[Muggle-Born Registration Commission]]. He may have been sent to [[Azkaban]], or else gone into hiding, like many others of his heritage.\n\n==Physical appearance==\nDonaghan was described as \"hairy\"<ref name=\"hbp30\" /> and, like all members of the Weird Sisters, wore \"artfully torn\" black robes when onstage.<ref name=\"GOF23\"/>\n\n==Behind the scenes==\n*Donaghan Tremlett was portrayed by [[Steve Mackey]] in {{GOF|FA}}.\n*Depending on his month of birth, he may have been in the same year as [[Charles Weasley]], [[Nymphadora Tonks]] and [[Jacob's sibling]], among others.\n\n==Appearances==\n{{Imagecat|Images of Donaghan Tremlett}}\n*{{PS|G}} {{FWC}}\n*{{COS|G}} {{FWC}}\n*{{GOF}} {{1st}}\n*{{GOF|F}}\n*{{OOTP}} {{Mention}}\n*{{HBP}}\n*{{JKRS}}\n*{{PM}} {{FWC}}\n*{{LEGO|Y1}} {{C|Named as \"Bassist\"}}\n*{{HM}} {{Mention}}\n\n==Notes and references==\n{{Reflist}}\n{{Start box}}\n{{Succession box\n|predecessor=[[Gwenog Jones]]\n|position=[[Wizard of the Month]]\n|years=[[July]] [[2004]]\n|successor=[[Honoria Nutcombe]]\n}}\n{{End box}}\n{{Weird Sisters}}\n{{Chocolate Frog Cards}}\n\n{{DEFAULTSORT:Tremlett, Donaghan}}\n\n[[de:Donaghan Tremlett]]\n[[de2:Donaghan Tremlett]]\n[[es:Donaghan Tremlett]]\n[[fr:Donaghan Tremlett]]\n[[fi:Donaghan Tremlett]]\n[[it:Donaghan Tremlett]]\n[[pl:Donaghan Tremlett]]\n[[ru:\u0414\u043e\u043d\u0430\u0433\u0430\u043d \u0422\u0440\u0435\u043c\u043b\u0435\u0442\u0442]]\n[[Category:1972 births]]\n[[Category:British or Irish individuals]]\n[[Category:Kenmare Kestrels supporters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Married individuals]]\n[[Category:Muggle-borns]]\n[[Category:Silver Chocolate Frog Cards]]\n[[Category:Sorted in the 1980s]]\n[[Category:Tremlett family]]\n[[Category:Unknown House]]\n[[Category:Weird Sisters]]\n[[Category:Wizard of the Month]]\n[[Category:Wizards]]"
