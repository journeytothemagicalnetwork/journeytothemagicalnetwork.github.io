sometime in the mid-[[2000s]].<ref name=\"p1-a2-s9\" /><ref name=\"p1-a2-s17\" /> He existed only in the [[alternate reality]] created by [[Albus Potter]] and [[Scorpius Malfoy]] [[Time-Turner|travelling back in time]] and changing the outcome of the first task of the [[Triwizard Tournament]].<ref name=\"p1-a2-s9\" /><ref name=\"p1-a2-s17\" /> In this universe, Ron married Padma instead of [[Hermione Granger]], since Hermione never dated [[Viktor Krum]], which in the main timeline made him jealous and prompted him to act on his feelings.<ref name=\"p1-a2-s9\" /><ref name=\"p1-a2-s17\" /><ref name=\"p1-a2-s16\">{{CC|B|2|16}}</ref>\n\nPanju attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]].<ref name=\"p1-a2-s9\" /><ref name=\"p1-a2-s17\" /> He bet his universe's version of his cousin [[Albus Potter]] that he couldn't get sorted into [[Gryffindor]].<ref name=\"p1-a2-s9\" /> This prompted Albus to convince the [[Sorting Hat]] to place him in Gryffindor to spite Panju.<ref name=\"p1-a2-s9\" />\n\nHe had a mischievous spirit and frequently got into trouble at Hogwarts.<ref name=\"p1-a2-s9\" /><ref name=\"p1-a2-s17\" /> According to his father, he usually laughed when confronted about his behaviour, and had a cocky smile that everyone wanted to wipe off his face.<ref name=\"p1-a2-s9\" /> When Panju landed in trouble at Hogwarts during the 2020-2021 school year, his father went to the school to talk to him in person instead of sending a [[Howler]], since his mother thought this would be more effective.<ref name=\"p1-a2-s9\" /> In another incident later that same school year, Panju ended up with no eyebrows and a \"rather large\" moustache after a mishap in [[Potions (class)|Potions]] class, prompting his father to once again pay a visit to Hogwarts to talk to him.<ref name=\"p1-a2-s17\" />\n\nRon's comments about Panju helped Albus and Scorpius realise that their actions had changed history and created an alternate
