It is unknown how a professor would be elected for the post, but it's most likely that the headmaster chooses the professor who embodies all the qualities [[Godric Gryffindor]] himself had (bravery, daring, conscientiousness, etc.) and was in Gryffindor House when he/she was at Hogwarts.<ref name=\"PS7\">{{PS|B|7}}</ref><ref name=\"OOTP11\">{{OOTP|B|11}}</ref>\n\nEvery portrait in [[Gryffindor Tower]] depicts a current or former Head of Gryffindor.<ref name=\"MHP\">{{MHP}} (see [http://www.flickr.com/photos/karen_roe/7366145410/in/set-72157630012281279/ this image])</ref>\n\n==History==\n[[File:200px-Minerva cs.jpg|thumb|left|160px|[[Minerva McGonagall]] - Head of [[Gryffindor]] during [[Harry Potter]]'s time at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]]]]\nThe head of house before and during Harry Potter's time was [[Minerva McGonagall]].<ref name=\"PS8\">{{PS|B|8}}</ref> Minerva became the head of house possibly when she was appointed [[Transfiguration (class)|Transfiguration]] professor at [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]], taking over Albus Dumbledore's previous teaching position when he became Headmaster.\n\n===Special duties===\nThe following are special duties that were performed by the Head of Gryffindor in the past.\n\n*[[1992\u20131993 school year]]\n**To inform the [[Gryffindor]] students of new rules regarding the opening of the [[Chamber of Secrets]].<ref name=\"COS14\">{{COS|B|14}}</ref>\n*[[1993\u20131994 school year]]\n**To notify their students of the rules in regards to protecting them from [[Azkaban]] escapee, [[Sirius Black]].<ref name=\"POA\">{{POA}}</ref>\n**To keep watch over the Gryffindors, along with the [[Prefect]]s whilst [[Hogwarts Castle]] was being searched for [[Sirius Black]].<ref name=\"POA\"/>\n*[[1997\u20131998 school year]]\n**To round up their students prior to the [[Battle of Hogwarts]].<ref name=\"DH30\">{{DH|B|30}}</ref>\n\n==Known
