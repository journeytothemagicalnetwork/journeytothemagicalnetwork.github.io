Both he and his wife participated in the [[First Wizarding War]], they likely fought against many [[Auror]]s and members of the [[Order of the Phoenix]].\n\n===After the war===\nOn [[Merula Snyde's tenth birthday|Merula's tenth birthday]], he and his wife gave Merula a broom as a present. They took turns taking Merula on the broom.
