was born somewhere in the [[Great Britain|United Kingdom]] between [[1 September]] [[2006|2005]] and [[31 August]] [[2007|2006]].<ref name=\"act1scene4\">{{CC|B|1|4}}</ref>\n\n===First year===\n{{Quote|Albus Potter, the Slytherin Squib.|Jenkins insulting Albus Potter's poor magical abilities|Harry Potter and the Cursed Child}}\nKarl began attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] on 1 September [[2017]].<ref name=\"act1scene4\" /> As the [[sorting ceremony]] was starting, he expressed wonder at the prospect of [[Albus Potter]], son of the famous [[Harry Potter]], being in his year.<ref name=\"act1scene4\" /> Karl was sorted into [[Gryffindor]] [[Hogwarts Houses|house]].<ref name=\"House\"/>\n\nWhen Albus Potter proved unable to get his [[broomstick]] to rise off the ground during their first [[flying (class)|flying]] lesson with [[Rolanda Hooch|Madam Hooch]], Karl teased him, calling him the \"[[Slytherin]] [[Squib]].\"<ref name=\"act1scene4\" />\n\n===Second year===\nDuring a [[second year|second-year]] [[Potions (class)|Potions]] lesson, Albus and [[Scorpius Malfoy]] had difficulty preparing their [[potion]], prompting Karl to tell the other students in the class to \"leave [Albus] and Voldemort\u2019s child to it.\"<ref name=\"act1scene4\" />\n\n===Fourth year===\nUpon returning to [[2020]] after [[Time-Turner|altering the past]],
