A [[Hufflepuff]] in his [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry|Hogwarts]] years, he rose to the office of [[Minister for Magic]], serving from [[1858]] to [[1865]].<ref name=\"pottermore\">{{PM}}</ref>\n\n==Biography==\n===Early life===\nDugald McPhail was born on an unknown date, no later than [[1841]],<ref>In order to have been appointed Minister for Magic in [[1858]], McPhail would have had to be out of Hogwarts and thus at least 17 at that point, placing his earliest birth year in [[1841]] \u2014 although it is unlikely someone that young would become Minister for Magic.</ref> to at least one magical parent. His surname, coupled with the fact that he spoke in a heavy Scottish brogue, implies he was from [[Scotland]], or had Scottish ancestry.\n\nIn his youth, he attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]], where he was [[Sorting ceremony|Sorted]] into [[Hufflepuff]].<ref name=\"pottermore\"/>\n\n===As Minister for Magic===\nMcPhail was appointed [[Minister for Magic]], sometime after leaving Hogwarts, from [[1858]] to [[1865]].<ref name=\"pottermore\"/>.  He was elected Minister at least once, perhaps twice.\n\nDuring his time as Minister, McPhail came up with the idea of mimicking the then-new [[Non-magic people|Muggle]] bus service to provide underage and infirm [[wizardkind|witches and wizards]] with a discreet method of transportation, leading to the introduction of the [[Knight Bus]] in [[1865]].<ref name=\"Knight Bus\">{{WW|the-knight-bus}}</ref>\n\nHis overall term in office was viewed as \"successful\" by [[Gabriel Truman]] in the [[1990s]].<ref name=\"pottermore\" />\n\n===Legacy===\nOn [[1 September]], [[1991]], [[Prefect]] [[Gabriel Truman]] welcomed the [[Hufflepuff]] [[first year]]s to [[Hufflepuff Basement]] with a short speech in which he mentioned former Minister Dugald McPhail as one of the many notable Hogwarts students who were [[Sorting ceremony|Sorted]], like them, into Hufflepuff.<ref name=\"pottermore\" />\n\n==Appearances==\n*{{PM}} {{1st Mention}}\n*{{WW}} {{Mention}}\n\n==Notes and references==\n{{Reflist}}\n\n{{Start box}}\n{{Succession box\n|predecessor=[[Priscilla Dupont]]\n|position=[[Minister for Magic]]\n|years=[[1858]]-[[1865]]\n|successor=[[Faris Spavin]]\n}}\n{{End box}}\n{{Minister for Magic}}\n\n{{DEFAULTSORT:McPhail, Dugald}}\n\n[[de:Dugald McPhail]]\n[[es:Dugald McPhail]]\n[[fr:Dugald McPhail]]\n[[pl:Dugald McPhail]]\n[[pt-br:Dugald McPhail]]\n[[ru:\u0414\u0443\u0433\u0430\u043b\u0434 \u041c\u0430\u043a\u0444\u044d\u0439\u043b]]\n[[zh:\u675c\u683c\u5fb7\u00b7\u9ea6\u514b\u8d39\u5c14]]\n[[Category:British Ministers for Magic]]\n[[Category:Historical figures]]\n[[Category:Hufflepuffs]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Scottish individuals]]\n[[Category:Wizards]]"
