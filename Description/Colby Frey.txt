They befriended [[Cassandra Vole]], and she led their group in bullying their fellow students including [[Daniel Page]].<ref name=\"MA\"/>\n\nThey went to the [[Hogwarts Library|Library]] one night to find a newspaper article about Daniel. Daniel, [[Ivy Warrington]] and their [[Unidentified 2000s Hogwarts student|friend]] found out that they had broken curfew and went to find out why.
