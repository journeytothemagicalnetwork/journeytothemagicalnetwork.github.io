"Rebecca was a Ravenclaw student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry during the 1990s. In 1992, the same year the Chamber of Secrets was opened, she became a member of the Duelling Club and was ranked nine on the Club's top-ten duellists list.The name \"Rebecca\" comes from the Hebrew name  (Rivqah), possibly meaning \"a snare\" in Hebrew, or perhaps derived from an Aramaic name.Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (video game) (First appearance)"
