"Aki Sugiyama was a witch and Hufflepuff student who attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s.She was seen wandering around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.Aki is a Japanese female given name which can mean \"autumn,\" \"bright,\" or \"sparkle\" depending on the character used to write it.This student was only featured in the console version of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (First appearance)"
