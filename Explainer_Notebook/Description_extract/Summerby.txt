"Summerby was a wizard who attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s.He was a Hufflepuff student who replaced Cedric Diggory as their Quidditch team's Seeker at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry after Diggory's tragic death.During a match against Gryffindor in 1996, he was beaten to the Golden Snitch by Ginny Weasley, although Ginny said it was partly because he had a cold and sneezed at the wrong time. Ginny Weasley replaced Harry Potter following his life-long ban from Quidditch by Hogwarts High Inquisitor Dolores Umbridge (although this ban was immediately lifted when Umbridge was removed from Hogwarts).Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (First appearance)Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game)"
