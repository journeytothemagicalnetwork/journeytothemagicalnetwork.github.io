"This witch attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1980s and was Sorted into either Slytherin or Ravenclaw house.During her time at Hogwarts, she befriended Erika Rath and another wizard. This girl during 19851986 school year At some point by the 19851986 school year, she became a member of her house's Quidditch team.She and other team members got into a confrontation with Jacob's sibling team, after Erika injured Orion Amari in a Quidditch friendly.After the match with Jacob's sibling's team, which ended in a brawl between the teams, this girl, along with the rest of the players, served detention under the supervision of Rolanda Hooch. The teams managed to prove their dedication to sportsmanship and co-operation and were not disqualified from the competition for the Hogwarts Quidditch Cup.Which house she is in depends on that of Jacob's sibling (chosen by the player). If Jacob's sibling is in Gryffindor, Hufflepuff or Slytherin, this girl is in Ravenclaw. If Jacob's sibling is in Ravenclaw, she is in Slytherin.Her position in the team is a Beater or a Chaser.Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery (First appearance)"
