"Eldon Pembroke was a Hufflepuff student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. During the 1996-1997 school year, he could usually be found in the Charms Classroom, and enjoyed playing Exploding Snap with other students.The name \"Eldon\" comes from a surname which was from a place name meaning \"Ella's hill\" in Old English.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (PS2 version only)Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game) (NDS version only)"
