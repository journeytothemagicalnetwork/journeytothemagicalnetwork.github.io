"Imogen Stretton was a Slytherin student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.She attended during the 19931994 school year and blocked access to an area of the school grounds along with Holden Ledbury in the 19961997 school year.The name \"Imogen\" was the name of a princess in the play Cymbeline (1609) by Shakespeare. He based her on a legendary character named Innogen, but the name was printed incorrectly and never corrected. The name Innogen is probably derived from Gaelic inghean meaning \"maiden\".Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (PS2 version only)Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game) (NDS version only)"
