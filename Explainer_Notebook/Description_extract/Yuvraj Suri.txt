"Yuvraj Suri was a wizard and Gryffindor student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s.During the 19961997 school year, he could usually be found in the Exterior Walkway, and traded various objects (Gobstones, chess pieces and Butterbeer) for Chocolate Frog Cards.Yuvraj is an Indian title for \"Crown Prince.\" It is a popular name for Sikh and Hindu men in India. Suri is a name which in Persian means \"red rose\", although it is also used in India.This particular student was only featured in the console versions of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban and the Nintendo DS version of Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (First appearance) (Console versions)Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game) (NDS version only)"
