"Trupti Kadam was a Slytherin student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. She was seen walking through the school corridors during the 1993-1994 school year.Trupti is a female given name derived from a Sanskrit word meaning \"satisfaction, satiety or contentment.\"This student was only featured in the PlayStation 2 version of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban.Given her name and place at Hogwarts, it is likely that although she must've born in the UK she was of Indian descent.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (PS2 version only)"
