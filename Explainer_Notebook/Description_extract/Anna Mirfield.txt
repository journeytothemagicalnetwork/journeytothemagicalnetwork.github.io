"Anna Mirfield was a Gryffindor student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. She gave Harry Potter some ground unicorn horn in exchange for a bottle of butterbeer during the 19961997 school year.This particular student was only featured in the console versions of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban and the NDS version of Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (First appearance) (Console versions)Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game) (NDS version only)"
