"Arjuna Balaji was a Ravenclaw student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He was seen walking around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.Arjuna means \"white, clear\" in Sanskrit. This is the name of a hero in Hindu texts, the son of the god Indra and the princess Kunti. Balaji is a name for the Hindu god Vishnu.This student was only featured in the PlayStation 2 version of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban.Based on his name, it can be presumed that he is of Indian descent.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (PS2 version only)"
