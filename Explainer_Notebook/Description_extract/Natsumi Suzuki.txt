"Natsumi Suzuki was a Hufflepuff student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s, a member of the Suzuki family. She attended Hogwarts during the 1993-1994 school year.The name Natsumi is a Japanese given name, meaning either \"summer\" and \"beautiful\" or \"vegetables\" and \"pick, pluck\", depending on which characters one uses to write the name with.Based on her name, she is most likely of Japanese descent.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (PS2 version only)"
