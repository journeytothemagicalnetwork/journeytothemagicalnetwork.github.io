"Gwendoline Hedgeflower was a witch who attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s and was Sorted in Hufflepuff house.She participated and fought alongside most of her fellow Hogwarts students in the Battle of Hogwarts against Lord Voldemort and his army. During the 19951996 school year, she appeared in Harry's vision of Voldemort at the King's Cross station. Later that year, she celebrated in the Entrance Courtyard as the Weasley twins departed from Hogwarts.During the 19971998 school year, Gwendoline continued to attend Hogwarts under Severus Snape's regime, implying that she was half-blood or pure-blood. Gwendoline observing as Draco Malfoy was asked to rejoin Voldemort's forces On 2 May, 1998, she participated in the Battle of Hogwarts. After the ousting of Severus Snape, Gwendoline was seen running up the Grand Staircase alongside Harry Potter. She stood next to Luna Lovegood and Draco Malfoy in the Entrance Courtyard during Voldemort's speech.Gwendoline Hedgeflower was portrayed by Rachel Lin in film adaptations Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix, Half-Blood Prince, and Deathly Hallows: Part 2.Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film) (First appearance)Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2"
