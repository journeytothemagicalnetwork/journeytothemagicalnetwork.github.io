"Abigail Pugh was a Slytherin student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. She was seen walking around the school during the 19931994 school year.The name \"Abigail\" comes from the Hebrew name  ('Avigayil) meaning \"my father is joy\".Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (PS2 version only)"
