"Winky Crockett was a Slytherin student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1940s. They were Captain of the Slytherin Quidditch team from 1940 to 1944.The name of this character is a tribute to Jordan Crockett, who worked as an art department production assistant on the first two Harry Potter films, and as a draughtsman on Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban.Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film) (Name appears on plaque)"
