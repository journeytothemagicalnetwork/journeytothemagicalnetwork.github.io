"Selina Moore was a witch and Slytherin student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s.Selina is the English spelling \"Celine,\" a short form of the French name Marcelline, which ultimately descends from the Roman name Marcus.Selina can also be a Latinized English variant of \"Selene,\" a Greek name that means \"moon\" and the name given to the Titaness who represented the Moon.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (First appearance)"
