"Professor Uric Oapley was a wizard that was depicted in a large painting that hung in Gryffindor Tower, at Hogwarts Castle. In his lifeftime, he was Head of Gryffindor House.In the painting, Oapley is depicted as playing a game of Wizard's Chess with Podric Piles, with Tobias Stump, standing, watching over the game, and Muldoon Cragg, in the background, absorbed in reading.
