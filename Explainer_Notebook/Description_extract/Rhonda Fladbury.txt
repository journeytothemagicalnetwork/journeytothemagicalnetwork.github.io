"Rhonda Fladbury was a witch and Hufflepuff student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s.Her name was seen on the Marauder's Map in the 19931994 school year. She was a member of the Duelling Club run by Helen Dawlish in the Middle Courtyard during the 19961997 school year.The name \"Rhonda\" was probably intended to mean \"good spear\" from Welsh rhon \"spear\" and da \"good\", but possibly influenced by the name of the Rhondda Valley in South Wales, which means \"noisy\". It has been in use only since the 20th century.This particular student was only featured in the console versions of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban and the Nintendo DS version of Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (console versions only) (First mentioned) (Name seen on Marauder's Map)Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game) (NDS version only) (First appearance)"
