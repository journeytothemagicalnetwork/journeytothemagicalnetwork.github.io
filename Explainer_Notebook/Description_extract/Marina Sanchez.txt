"Marina Sanchez was a witch and a student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1980s.Marina Sanchez started attending Hogwarts on 1 September 1984 and was Sorted into an unknown House.During her third year, Marina took Care of Magical Creatures as one of her electives.During the 19891990 school year, her sixth year, Marina proceeded to take N.E.W.T. level Charms, Herbology, Transfiguration, History of Magic and Care of Magical Creatures.Her House is the same as that of Jacob's sibling, which is chosen by the player in the game.Marina replaces Rowan Khanna on the Leaderboard and classes after Year 6 Chapter 18.Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery (First appearance)"
