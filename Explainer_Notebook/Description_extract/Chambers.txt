"Chambers was a witch or wizard and Ravenclaw student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.Chambers played as Chaser for the Ravenclaw Quidditch team during the 19951996 school year. In the Quidditch final that year Ronald Weasley deflected a goal attempt by Chambers.\"Chambers\" is a surname that arrived in England with the Norman Conquest. It was an occupational surname for a servant in the bedroom of a nobleman.Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (First appearance)"
