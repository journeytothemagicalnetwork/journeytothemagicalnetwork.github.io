"Aamir Loonat was a Gryffindor student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He was seen walking around during the 19931994 school year.Aamir is a male given name of Indian origin, meaning \"full\", \"populous\" and \"prosperous\". Also used to refer to a prince, or ruler. The meaning of 'Loonat' is unknown.This student was only featured in the PlayStation 2 version of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban.Based on his name, it can be presumed he is of Indian descent.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (PS2 version only)"
