"Yatin Bhagat was a Slytherin student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He was seen walking around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.Yatin is an Indian male given name, meaning \"ascetic, devotee\". Bhagat is a Punjabi and Gujarati surname, meaning \"devotee\". In Sikhism and Hinduism, a Bhagat is a holy person who leads humanity towards God.This student was only featured in the PlayStation 2 version of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban.Based on his name, he is most likely of Indian descent, probably originating from the north-west of India.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (PS2 version only)"
