"Katsuji Yamazaki was a Hufflepuff student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He was seen walking around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.Katsuji is a Japanese given name, meaning \"victorious\". Yamazaki is a Japanese surname from a common place name meaning \"mountain promontory\".Given his name, it can be presumed that he is of Japanese descent.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (PS2 version only)"
