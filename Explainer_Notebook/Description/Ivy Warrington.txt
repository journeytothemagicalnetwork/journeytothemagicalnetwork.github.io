She had a [[Warrington sisters' grandmother|grandmother]] who was a witch, and one sister, [[Winifred Warrington]].<ref name=\"MA\"/>\n\nAt one point during her childhood, Winifred [[Vanishment|vanished]] herself. Their grandmother tried to help her forget what happened by using a [[Memory Charm]]. However, it caused her to wholly forget about Winifred. Ivy had an ability to vanish, and would do so even when she didn't intend to.<ref name=\"MA\"/>\n\n===Hogwarts years {{C|2008\u2013}}===\nAt the age of eleven, Ivy began attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]]. During the [[Sorting Ceremony]], Ivy was at first missing, having been distracted by bushes outside the castle, but was later found by [[Rubeus Hagrid]].<ref name=\"MA\"/>\n\n==Magical abilities and skills==\n*'''[[Charm]]s''': Ivy was proficient in the casting of charms. She could successfully cast the [[Levitation Charm]], the [[Disarming Charm]] and the [[Locking Spell]], for example.<ref name=\"MA\"/> She was also capable of producing a corporeal [[Patronus Charm|Patronus]] in the form of a [[pekingese]], an exceptional [[magic]]al feat for a Hogwarts student.<ref name=\"info\"/> She was known to enjoy [[Charms (class)|Charms class]] at Hogwarts.<ref name=\"info\"/>\n*'''[[Transfiguration]]''': She could successfully cast the [[Vanishing Spell]], to the point that she was able to [[Vanishment|vanish]] large objects, like luggage, adult-sized [[Suit of armour|suits of armours]] and even people.<ref name=\"MA\"/>\n*'''[[Healing magic]]''': She once successfully used the [[healing spell]] ''[[Episkey]]'' to heal [[Daniel Page]]'s broken nose.<ref name=\"MA\">{{MA}}</ref>\n\n==Possessions==\n*'''[[Ivy Warrington's wand|Wand]]''': Ivy's wand was 12\u00bd inches, made of [[Cherry (tree)|cherry]] with a [[dragon heartstring]] [[Wand core|core]].<ref name=\"info\"/>\n\n==Behind the scenes==\n*It is possible that she was related to [[Cassius Warrington]].\n*Ivy doesn't have a set House at Hogwarts.
