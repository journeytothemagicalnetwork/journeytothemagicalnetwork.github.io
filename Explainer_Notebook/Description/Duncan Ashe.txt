He put even [[Tulip Karasu|Tulip]] an' [[Nymphadora Tonks|Tonks]] ter shame.|[[Rubeus Hagrid]] regarding Duncan's mischief-making|Harry Potter: Hogwarts Mystery}}\n'''Duncan Ashe''' was a [[Wizardkind|wizard]] who attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in the second half of the 20th century and was [[Sorting ceremony|Sorted]] into [[Slytherin]].\n\n==Biography==\nHe was friends with [[Jacob (Hogwarts student)|Jacob]] from his first day at school until his dying day. The two were noted to be mischief makers by others, and frequently visited [[Zonko's Joke Shop]]. At some point, he and Jacob also broke their [[Broomstick|brooms]].<ref name=\"HM57\">{{HM|5|7}}</ref> He was also friends with the school [[poltergeist]], [[Peeves]].<ref name=\"HM522\">{{HM|5|22}}</ref> Sometime during the [[1970s]] or [[1980s]], he died when compelled by [[R]] to concoct an [[Erumpent Potion]], which exploded while he was preparing it. After his death, Duncan returned as a [[ghost]] and stayed at Hogwarts, though he rarely showed himself to the other residents of the Castle.<ref name=\"HM56\">{{HM|5|6}}</ref>\n\nHe helped Jacob's sibling appease Peeves by unleashing various forms of chaos upon the school in order to persuade Peeves to hand over the [[Vault portrait]].<ref name=\"HM522\"/>\n\n==Personality and traits==\nWhen he was alive, Duncan loved making mischief and was noted to be a mischief maker by others along with his friend, [[Jacob (Hogwarts student)|Jacob]]. After he died, he isolated himself and rarely showed himself to the other residents of the Castle. He was angry and resentful at Jacob for taking the blame for [[R]] and joining them.\n\n==Magical abilities and skills==\n*'''[[Potion]]s''': Duncan was adept in the art of making potions, as he made a powerful [[Erumpent Potion]].<ref name=\"HM56\"/>\n*'''[[Flight|Flying]]''': [[Rolanda Hooch|Madam Hooch]] described Duncan Ashe as an \"excellent flier\", meaning he was highly skilled with flying on a [[broomstick]].<ref name=\"HM57\"/>\n\n==Appearances==\n{{Imagecat|Images of Duncan Ashe}}\n*{{HM}} {{1st}} {{Ghost}}\n\n==Notes and references==\n{{Reflist}}\n{{DEFAULTSORT:Ashe, Duncan}}\n{{Start box}}\n{{Succession box\n|predecessor=[[Peeves]] (possibly)\n|years=c.
