It is ultimately derived from Old English stig \"house\" and weard \"guard\". As a given name, it arose in [[19th century]] [[Scotland]] in honour of the Stuart royal family, which produced several kings and queens of Scotland and Britain between the 14th and 18th centuries.<ref>http://www.behindthename.com/name/stuart</ref>\n*\"Ackerley\" is a Middle English name meaning 'aker', a division of land + 'ley', a lea or
