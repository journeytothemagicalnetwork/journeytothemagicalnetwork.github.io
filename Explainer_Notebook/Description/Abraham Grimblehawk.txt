He was the uncle of [[Mathilda Grimblehawk]].\n\n==Biography==\nAbraham attended [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] and was placed in [[Hufflepuff|Hufflepuff House]]. He most likely studied [[Herbology]] and [[Care of Magical Creatures]] at Hogwarts.<ref name=\"FBC\" />\n\nAbraham worked in the [[British Ministry of Magic]], but it is unknown in which department.
