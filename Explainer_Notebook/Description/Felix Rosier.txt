In [[1984]], he had been made a [[Prefect]], and was responsible for watching over the [[first year]]s. He graduated from Hogwarts in [[1987]].\n\n==Biography==\n\n===Early life {{C|1968/69-1980}}===\nFelix was born in the late [[1960s]]. His father may have been a top [[Death Eaters|Death Eater]] killed or imprisoned during the [[First Wizarding War]].<ref name=\"Death Eater father\">{{HM|3|3}}</ref>\n\n===Hogwarts years {{C|1980-1987}}===\nFelix started his education in [[1980]] and was sorted into [[Slytherin]]. He cared about the [[House Cup]] and having Slytherin in the lead. Sometimes this meant breaking the rules, such as using the [[Freezing Charm]] on [[Slytherin Quidditch team]]'s opponents to help Slytherin win the [[Inter-House Quidditch Cup|Quidditch Cup]].<ref name=\"Y3PrefectSQ\" />\n\nIn his [[fifth year]], Felix became the Slytherin male [[Prefect]].\n\n===After Hogwarts {{C|1987-}}===\nFelix later went on to become a [[dragonologist]] after graduating from Hogwarts and specialised in [[Peruvian Vipertooth]]s.\n[[File:Prefect Convention.jpg|200px|thumb|left|Felix getting together with the other ex-prefects]]\nAt some point, Bill Weasley had contacted Felix and the other prefects for a get-together at the [[Three Broomsticks Inn|Three Broomsticks]] pub in Hogsmeade.\n\n==Magical abilities and skills==\n*'''[[Care of Magical Creatures]]''': Felix was knowledgeable and skilled in the field of [[Magizoology]], the study of [[magic]]al [[creature]]s. After graduating from Hogwarts, Felix became a [[Dragonologist]] and specialised in [[Peruvian Vipertooth]]s.\n*'''[[Charm]]s''': Felix possessed a degree of talent in charm-work, being able to successfully use the [[Freezing Charm]] to immobilise [[Quidditch]] opponents of the [[Slytherin Quidditch team]] surreptitiously.<ref name=\"Y3PrefectSQ\" /> He was also skilled with the [[Knockback Jinx]].<ref name=\"HM2SQ\">{{HM|2|Slytherin Schemes}}</ref>\n*'''[[Potion]]s''': Felix was proficient in potion-making, as he was capable of successfully brewing [[Babbling Beverage]].<ref
