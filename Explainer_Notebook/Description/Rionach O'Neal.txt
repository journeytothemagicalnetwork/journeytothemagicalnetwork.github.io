She had long dark brown hair, which she kept in two {{w|French braid}}s during the [[1995\u20131996 school year]]. She was tall and willowy.\n\n==Etymology==\nRionach is a variant of the Irish ''R\u00edoghnach'', which means \"queen\". O'Neal is a form of the Irish [[Gaelic]] ''Ua N\u00e9ill'', meaning \"grandson/descendant of Niall\".\n\n==Behind the scenes==\n*Rionach O'Neal was portrayed by [[Marianne Chase]] in the [[Harry Potter (film series)|''Harry Potter'' films]]. However, her Mandy profile was later updated to state she played the same character in her subsequent appearances.<ref name=\"appearances\" />\n*What Rionach did in every film is unknown, apart from her confirmed appearance in them.
