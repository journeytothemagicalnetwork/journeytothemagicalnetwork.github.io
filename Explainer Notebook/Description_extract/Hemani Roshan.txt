"Hemani Roshan was a Ravenclaw student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s.She gave Harry Potter some powdered moonstone in exchange for two chess pieces. After then she frequently said, \"Thanks for the chess pieces, Harry.\" She could be found hanging around on the Wooden Bridge.Hermani is an Indian name meaning \"made of gold\", and is a name for Parvati. Roshan means \"Light\" in various sanskirt languages.This student was only featured in the console versions of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban.Based on her name, it can be presumed that she is of Indian descent.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (First appearance) (Console versions)Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game) (NDS version only)"
