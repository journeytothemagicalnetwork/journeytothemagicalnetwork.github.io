"Andrew Cleveley was Gryffindor student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He was seen walking around the school during the 19931994 school year.The name \"Andrew\" comes from the Greek name  (Andreas), which was derived from  (aner) \"man\" (genitive  (andros) \"of a man\"). This name has been common (in various spellings) throughout the Christian world, and it became very popular in the Middle Ages. Saint Andrew is regarded as the patron of Scotland, Russia, Greece and Romania.
