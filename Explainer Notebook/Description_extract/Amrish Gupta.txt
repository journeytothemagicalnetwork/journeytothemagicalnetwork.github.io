"Amrish Gupta was a Slytherin student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He was seen walking around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.Amrish is a male given name, meaning \"Lord Indra\". Gupta is a North India surname, most likely derived from the Sanskrit \"goptri\" meaning \"military governor\". Another more direct translation from Sanskrit would be \"secret\" or \"hidden\".Based on his name, he is most likely of Indian descent.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (PS2 version only)"
