"Raashid Hussain was a Hufflepuff student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He was seen walking around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.Raashid is an Arabic name meaning \"rightly guided\". Hussain is an Arabic surname and given name and means \"handsome\".
