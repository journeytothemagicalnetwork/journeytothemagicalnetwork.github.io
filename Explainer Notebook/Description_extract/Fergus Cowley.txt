"Fergus Cowley was a wizard and Slytherin student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s.He was a member of the Duelling Club run by Maynard Hatton in the Training Grounds during the 19961997 school year.The name \"Fergus\" means \"man of vigour\", derived from the Gaelic elements fear \"man\" and gus \"vigour\".Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (First appearance) (Console versions)Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game) (NDS version only)"
