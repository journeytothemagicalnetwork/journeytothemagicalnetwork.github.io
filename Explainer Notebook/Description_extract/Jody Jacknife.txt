"Jody Jacknife was a Slytherin student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1960s. She was Captain of the Slytherin Quidditch team from 1960 to 1964.This character was presumably named after Jody Jackman, an art department coordinator on every Harry Potter film, excepting Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone.Harry Potter films (Name appears on plaque)"
