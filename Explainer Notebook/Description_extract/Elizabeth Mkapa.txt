"Elizabeth Mkapa was a Ravenclaw student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. She was seen walking around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.Elizabeth is derived from the Hebrew 'Elisheva', which means \"my God is an oath\". The surname Mkapa is Tanzanian, borne by Benjamin Mkapa, the third president of Tanzania, and probably indicates an African ancestry.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (PS2 version only)"
