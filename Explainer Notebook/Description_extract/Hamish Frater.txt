"Hamish Frater was a wizard and a former Captain for the Gryffindor Quidditch team.By the early 1990s, there was a plaque on the mantlepiece at Gryffindor Tower listing him as a former Gryffindor Quidditch Captain.Hamish Frater is a video game artist who worked for PlayStation Home. Pottermore for PlayStation Home was the last thing he worked on before leaving Sony; thus this naming is a tribute to him.Pottermore (Playstation Home)"
