"Grant Page was a wizard and Ravenclaw student who attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He was the Keeper for the Ravenclaw Quidditch team during the 19931994 school year.The name \"Grant\" comes from a Scottish surname which was derived from Norman French grand meaning \"great, large\".Harry Potter: Quidditch World Cup (First appearance)"
