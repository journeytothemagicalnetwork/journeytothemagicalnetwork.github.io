"Adrian (b. 1959 or 1960) was a wizard who worked as a photographer. During his school years at Hogwarts, he was a member of the Slug Club.Adrian was born in either 1959 or 1960. He began attending Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in 1971, and, at some point, was made a member of the Slug Club by his Potions Master, Horace Slughorn. Slughorn had high expectations on the future of this student. Adrian eventually graduated in 1978. Adrian and Dean Thomas at Slughorn's Christmas party Adrian grew up to be a photographer, to Slughorn's disappointment, as he hoped more of him. The professor still maintained, however, a good relationship with him.On 20 December, 1996, Adrian attended a Christmas party at Hogwarts Castle, held by his former professor, Horace Slughorn. Slughorn introduced Harry Potter to him, and he took a picture of Harry and Slughorn together. By March of the following year, Slughorn kept this picture on the front and centre of \"the shelf.\"Several saints and six popes have borne this name, including the only English pope, Adrian IV, and the only Dutch pope, Adrian VI. As an English name, it has been in use since the Middle Ages, though it was not popular until modern times.In LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7, he was credited as \"Daily Prophet Photographer\".As he joined Hogwarts in 1971, he would have been sorted the same time as Severus Snape, Lily Evans and the Marauders.Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film) (First appearance)LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7 (DS version, as \"Daily Prophet Photographer\")Harry Potter: The Character Vault"
