"Ashok Khanna was a wizard and Ravenclaw student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He was seen walking around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.Ashok is a common name in India that means \"not causing sorrow.\" Khanna is an Indian surname based on the name of a Khatri clan from the Punjab region who are said to be descended from a khan. This student was only featured in the console version of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban.Based on his name, it can be presumed that he is of Indian descent.Due to the games being released independently, it is not specified if he is related to Rowan Khanna. However, Rowan mentions having a brother in passing, and Ashok is in an appropriate age range to fit the possibility of being said brother.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (First appearance)"
