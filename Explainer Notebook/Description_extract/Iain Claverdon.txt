"Iain Claverdon was a wizard and Gryffindor student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s.He owned two sets of Quidditch robes, one of which he agreed to give to Harry Potter before the final match between Gryffindor and Ravenclaw in 1997, in exchange for five Ever-Bashing Boomerangs. Harry gave the robes to Dean Thomas in order to replace the ones he dirtied in a duel with some Slytherins.This particular student was only featured in the home console versions of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban and the Nintendo DS version of Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (First appearance) (Console versions only)Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game) (NDS version only)"
