"Barry Rochester was a Gryffindor student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He was seen walking around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.This student was only featured in the PlayStation 2 version of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (PS2 version only)"
