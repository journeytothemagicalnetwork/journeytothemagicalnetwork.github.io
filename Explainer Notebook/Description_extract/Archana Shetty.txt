"Archana Shetty was a Slytherin student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. She was seen walking around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.Archana is a female given name of Indian origin, it means \"worship\". Shetty is an Indian surname, mainly found in the state of Karnataka.Based on her name, she is most likely of Indian descent.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (PS2 version only)"
