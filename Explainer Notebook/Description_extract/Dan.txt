"Dan was a British wizard who had a portrait of himself hung somewhere in Hogwarts Castle by the 1991-1992 school year. Dan attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in his youth, and was sorted into Gryffindor House, and wore the Gryffindor Lion emblem on his long brown cloak which we wore as an adult, as shown in his portrait.This portrait appeared in the PC version of the video game adaptation of Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone.
