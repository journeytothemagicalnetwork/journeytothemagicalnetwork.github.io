"Ivan Renshaw was a student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s.He competed in the school Gobstones tournament during the 19961997 school year.The name \"Ivan\" is the newer form of the old Slavic name  (Ioannu), which was derived from Greek Ioannes. It refers back to John, which means \"God (Herbrew) is gracious\".Renshaw is a surname from Prestbury, Cheshire in England. The suffix shaw means \"wood\",so the whole name means \"Ren-wood\".Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (video game) (Nintendo DS version only)"
