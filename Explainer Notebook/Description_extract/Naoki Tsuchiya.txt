"Naoki Tsuchiya was a Gryffindor student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He was seen walking around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.Naoki is Japanese, and means  (nao) \"honest, straight\" and  (ki) \"tree\". Tsuchiya is a Japanese surname, meaning \"earthen house\".This student was only featured in the PlayStation 2 version of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban.Based on his name, he is most likely of Japanese descent.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (PS2 version only)"
