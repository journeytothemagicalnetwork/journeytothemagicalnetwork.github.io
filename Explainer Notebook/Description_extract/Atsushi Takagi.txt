"Atsushi Takagi was a wizard and Slytherin student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the 1990s. He was seen walking around the school during the 1993-1994 school year.Atsushi is a male name of Japanese origin. Takagi is a common Japanese surname, meaning \"tall tree\".Based on his name, it can be presumed that he is of Japanese descent.Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (video game) (First appearance)"
