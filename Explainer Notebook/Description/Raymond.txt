He was accepted into [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] and was [[Sorting ceremony|sorted]] into [[Ravenclaw|Ravenclaw House]].<ref name=\"PSG\"/>\n\n===Cat in the bottom of the well===\n[[Harry Potter]] first met Raymond in the Hogwarts Grounds, in [[1991]]. The boy was sitting next to a well and when Harry went closer, he told him that [[Raymond's kitten|his kitten]] fell into the well. Later, Harry was in a [[Potions (class)|potions]] side-room and a cloaked [[Quirinus Quirrell]] blasted him in a potions storeroom with the ''[[Deprimo]]'' [[charm]]. After defeating some [[Troll]]s Harry went into the bottom of the well and saw Raymond on top. That was the first time he mentioned his name to Harry.
