He had been made a [[Prefect]] in [[1984]] and was responsible for watching over the [[first year]] students in Ravenclaw.<ref name=\"HM13\">{{HM|1|3}} {{c|info revealed to [[Ravenclaw]] players}}</ref> Chester was still at Hogwarts the following two years and graduated in [[1987]].<ref name=\"graduation\" />\n\n==Biography==\nChester Davies was born between [[1 September]] [[1968]] and [[31 August]] [[1969]].<ref name=\"age\" /> He had a younger brother, [[Roger Davies]], born about nine years later.<ref name=\"brother\" />\n\n===Hogwarts years {{c|1980-1987}}===\nHe began attending [[Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry]] in [[September]] [[1980]] and was placed in [[Ravenclaw|Ravenclaw House]] by the [[Sorting Hat]]. He became a [[Prefect]] in [[1984]].\n\nDuring the [[1986\u20131987 school year]], Chester was caught off guard by a [[Full Body-Bind Curse]] when he was in front of the [[Potions Classroom]]. It was unknown if he was aware that the [[curse]] was cast by [[Jacob's sibling]] with the coercion of [[Penny Haywood]], or who had revived him afterwards.<ref name=\"HM3P\">{{HM|3|Penny for Your Thoughts}}</ref> Later in the school year, when [[Rita Skeeter|a guest speaker]] was to arrive at Hogwarts, Chester believed the person to be [[Ambrosius Flume]], the owner of [[Honeydukes]] Sweet Shoppe.<ref name=\"HM3W\">{{HM|3|Wizarding World Famous}}</ref>\n\nChester graduated from Hogwarts in [[1987]].<ref name=\"age\" />\n\n===Later life {{c|1987-}}===\n[[File:Chester Davies Ministry employee.png|thumb|left|Chester Davies as a Ministry employee, during the [[1989\u20131990 school year]]]]\nBy [[1988]], Chester started working in the [[Improper Use of Magic Office]] at the [[British Ministry of Magic]].<ref name=\"Y5SQ\">{{HM|5|Yer a Prefect!}}</ref>\n\nIn [[September]] 1988, he met up in the [[Three Broomsticks Inn]] with other former Prefects [[Angelica Cole]], [[Jane Court]] and [[Felix Rosier]], as well as Prefects at the time [[William Weasley]], [[Charles Weasley]] and Jacob's sibling.<ref name=\"Y5SQ\"/>\n\nIn the [[1989\u20131990 school year]], he met up with
